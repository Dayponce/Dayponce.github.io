About me

Hi, I'm Daisy! I have a coral research background and hold a Master's degree in Marine Science (M.S.) from NOVA Southeastern University. As a marine scientist and data analyst, I posses a strong academic foundation and experience in using various tools and technologies to collect, organize, and analyze data to inform business decisions. My proficiency lies in R, SQL, Excel, and Tableau, and have developed a keen understanding of data analytics techniques, including dashboard building, report & proposal writing, data mining, data merging, statistics, critical thinking and scientific communincation. Within this portfolio, you will discover a projects that exemplify both my data analytics and research skills. Among these examples is my Master's research and other projects, showcasing my expertise in statistical analysis. These projects demonstrate my ability to analyze and present data in a clear and visually appealing way, making it easy for decision-makers to understand the insights and take action.

Through my research, I have cultivated a deep excitement and curiosity for data analysis. I find joy in challenging my understanding of data, unraveling the mysteries of the world, and making data-driven decisions. My passion for data and analytics drives me to contribute to meaningful research and conservation efforts in marine science, and I am eager to explore further roles aligned with this passion.
